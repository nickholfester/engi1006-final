My favorite class this semester:

My favorite class this semester was Fourier Analysis which was an indepth and rigorous study of the theory behind Fourier Series and Fourier Transformations. The subject of Fourier Transforms
and Fourier Series in itself is extremely interesting, but this course also acted as a synthesis of many math subjects such as complex analysis, functional analysis, and group theory, as well as many
physical topics, such as heat transfer, wave mechanics, and the schrodinger equation, at the same time. It was a whirlwind trip through many fundamental workings of the world around us and
I hope to find more classes like this during the rest of my time at Columbia.
